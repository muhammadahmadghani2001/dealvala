DealVala — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: November 3, 2025

Delete your account from within the app

You can permanently delete your DealVala account and associated personal data at any time, directly in the app:

  1. Open the DealVala app.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

What gets deleted

  • Your authentication record (email/ID) stored with our auth provider.
  • Profile fields (name, preferences if any).
  • App data linked to your user ID (e.g., saved deals, alerts).

What may be retained

  • Non-identifiable, aggregated analytics (e.g., total views or clicks not tied to a user).
  • System logs and backups for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance kept for up to 30 days before routine purge.

If you can’t access the app

If you’re unable to log in but want your account deleted, email us from your registered email address:

support@dealvala.com

Please include: your email address used in DealVala and a short note requesting deletion. We will verify ownership and complete deletion.

Partial deletion

We do not currently support partial deletion requests (e.g., delete some fields but keep the account). Deleting your account removes your personal data as described above.
